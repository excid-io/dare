Context definitions for the DARE project

Building

This represents a building where smart devices have been installed

Meter

This is a metering device. It can belong to one of the following categories:

  • building-level-meter It performs measurements at the building level.
  • floor-level-meter It performs measurements at the floor level.
  • hvac It performs measurements related to the hvac equipment of a building.
  • light It performs measurements related to the lighting equipment of a building.

Category

The value of that attributes corresponds to the category of a metering device

AirQualitySensor

This is an air quality measuring device.

gas-carbon-dioxide

The value of that attributes corresponds to a CO2 measurement of the air quality measuring device.

generic-volatile-organic-compound

The value of that attributes corresponds to a generic volatile organic compound PPM measurement of the air quality measuring device.

installedIn

The value of that attributes corresponds to a the building identifier in which a metering device or an air quality sensor has been installed.
