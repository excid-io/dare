About

The Data spAces for smaRt Energy (DARE) project aims to provide new, secure means for sharing data generated by IoT devices. DARE technology will enable IoT device owners to share data generated by their devices with 3rd party service providers. DARE integrates novel data semantics interoperability technologies, security and access control solutions.

DARE will demonstrate the developed technologies through a smart energy management proof of concept.
